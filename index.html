About Me

I'm 17 years old, a High School Senior

My name is Cameron Ythan King. I attend school at Little Red Schoolhouse and Elisabeth Irwin High School (LREI), located in SoHo, New York. I am a Prep for Prep alum and All Star Code alum. I live in Brooklyn, New York. I consider myself an athelte, a musician, a comedian, an artist, a designer and I am an aspiring Computer Engineer.

I was LREI's 2015 Athlete of the Year. In addition to playing three Varsity Sports( Soccer, Basketball, and Track and Field) and playing four instruments ( Piano, Drums, Trumpet, and Baritone Horn), I paint and I code. For example, I made this website on my own.

I have traveled to France with the Student Diplomacy Corps scholarship. I recently represented LREI at the White Privilege Conference in Louisville, Kentucky and help led a Black Lives Matter protest with my school. This summer, I participated in New York City's "Ladders for Leaders" program. I am currently planning a large New York City hackathon for Local Hack Day on October 10th, with alum from ASC and Girls Who Code. I intern for Bleu Magazine and I attended DroidCon, SpaceApps and PennApps.

S.M.A.R.T. Alarm Clock

I created an alarm clock that connected with my house's wifi to automatically go off and create alarms using the Google Calendar api. This was done using an Arduino Yun, a USB speaker, and a TFT touch screen sheild. The device could also set an alarm via email or sms text message.

Over the past of a few years, I've built many robots using the Lego Mindstorms products. I've made a sodoku solving robot, many variants on the classic line follow, a segway, a guitar, remote control car, and also a rubik's cube solving robot.
